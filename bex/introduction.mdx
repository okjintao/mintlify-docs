## Key Features

### Native to Berachain

BEX is built directly into Berachain's core protocol, offering:

- **Gas Efficiency**: Optimized for minimal transaction costs
- **Deep Integration**: Direct connection to PoL consensus
- **High Performance**: Fast execution with minimal slippage
- **Native Tokens**: Direct support for BERA, BGT, and HONEY

### Proof-of-Liquidity Powered

As part of PoL, BEX liquidity providers:

<Check>Earn BGT token emissions</Check>
<Check>Participate in network governance</Check>
<Check>Receive trading fee rewards</Check>
<Check>Contribute to network security</Check>

### Multiple Pool Types

BEX supports various pool types for different needs:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Constant Product Pools (x*y=k)">
    Traditional AMM pools suitable for most token pairs. Best for standard trading pairs with moderate volatility.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Stable Pools">
    Optimized for stablecoin and pegged asset pairs with minimal price impact and low slippage.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Weighted Pools">
    Multi-asset pools with customizable weights, ideal for index tokens and diversified exposure.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Concentrated Liquidity">
    Capital-efficient pools where LPs can provide liquidity in specific price ranges.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## BEX vs Other DEXs

| Feature | BEX | Other DEXs |
|---------|-----|------------|
| Chain Integration | Native | External dApp |
| PoL Rewards | Yes (BGT) | No |
| Gas Costs | Ultra-low | Variable |
| Transaction Speed | ~5 seconds | 12+ seconds |
| Governance | Direct (via BGT) | Separate token |

## Trading Pairs

BEX supports a wide range of trading pairs:

### Major Pairs
- BERA/HONEY
- BERA/wBTC
- BERA/wETH
- HONEY/USDC

### Ecosystem Pairs
- Various token pairs from Berachain ecosystem projects
- LP tokens and derivative assets
- Governance and protocol tokens

<Tip>
  New pairs are constantly being added based on community governance votes.
</Tip>

## Fee Structure

BEX employs a dynamic fee structure:

| Pool Type | Trading Fee | LP Share | Protocol Share |
|-----------|------------|----------|----------------|
| Standard | 0.3% | 0.25% | 0.05% |
| Stable | 0.04% | 0.03% | 0.01% |
| Volatile | 1.0% | 0.85% | 0.15% |

<Note>
  Fee percentages can be adjusted through governance proposals.
</Note>
