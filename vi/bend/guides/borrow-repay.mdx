This guide will walk you through the process of providing collateral to borrow $HONEY, and repaying the loan to avoid liquidation.

## Requirements

- Wallet with collateral (WETH, WBERA, WBTC, etc) listed on [Bend](https://bend.berachain.com/borrow)
- Native $BERA to process transactions

<Tip>
If you do not have any of the collateral listed, you can use [Berachain Hub](https://hub.berachain.com/swap).
</Tip>

## Supplying Collateral & Borrowing

This will walk you through the steps of interacting with Bend, supplying $WBERA as collateral to take out a loan in $HONEY.

### 1. Visit Bend & Connect Wallet

Your first step is to go to [Bend](https://bend.berachain.com/borrow) and connect your wallet.

![Berachain Bend - Borrow & Connect Wallet](/images/bend-borrow-connect-wallet.png)

### 2. Choose A Market

Choose a market that you can offer as collateral.

For this guide, we'll be using $WBERA.

![Berachain Bend - Choose Market](/images/bend-borrow-choose-market.png)

### 3. Configure Borrow

1. Make sure to select **Borrow**
2. Specify a $WBERA amount you'd like to deposit
3. Click **Review** to bring up a modal to confirm

![Berachain Bend - Configure Borrow](/images/bend-borrow-configure-borrow.png)

### 4. Approve & Supply

1. Approve transfer of the $WBERA token to the market
2. Supply the $WBERA to the market as collateral

![Berachain Bend - Approve & Supply](/images/bend-borrow-approve-supply.png)

![Berachain Bend - Successful Approve & Supply](/images/bend-borrow-successful-approve-supply.png)

You should now see the successfully supplied collateral and total value in the market.

![Berachain Bend - Successful Supply](/images/bend-borrow-successful-supply.png)

### 5. Borrow

1. Specify a $HONEY amount to borrow
2. Ensure that the borrow is below the LTV limit to avoid liquidation
3. Click **Review** to bring up a modal to confirm

![Berachain Bend - Borrow](/images/bend-borrow-borrow.png)

### 6. Approve & Borrow

Approve the borrow of $HONEY.

![Berachain Bend - Approve Borrow](/images/bend-borrow-approve-borrow.png)

![Berachain Bend - Successful Approve Borrow](/images/bend-borrow-successful-approve-borrow.png)

You should now see the successfully borrowed $HONEY and its value reflected in the market.

![Berachain Bend - Successful Borrow](/images/bend-borrow-successful-borrow.png)

### 7. Avoid Liquidation

Monitor your position carefully to ensure it is not liquidated if your LTV exceeds the Liquidation Loan-To-Value (LLTV).

![Berachain Bend - Monitor LTV/LLTV](/images/bend-borrow-monitor-ltv-lltv.png)

## Repay & Withdraw

We will now cover the process of repaying your loan with $HONEY and withdrawing your collateral in $WBERA.

### 1. Configure Repay

1. Select the **Repay** section
2. Specify the amount of $HONEY you wish to repay
3. Specify the amount of $WBERA collateral you wish to withdraw
4. Click **Review** to open a confirmation modal

![Berachain Bend - Configure Repay](/images/bend-repay-configure-repay.png)

### 2. Confirm Repay

Confirm the repayment transaction.

![Berachain Bend - Confirm Repay](/images/bend-repay-confirm-repay.png)

![Berachain Bend - Successful Confirm Repay](/images/bend-repay-successful-confirm-repay.png)

You should now see the successful repayment showing a zero balance.

![Berachain Bend - Successful Repay](/images/bend-repay-successful-repay.png)

You have now successfully borrowed and repaid a $HONEY loan on Bend.
