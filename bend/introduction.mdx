## What is Bend?

Bend is Berachain's native lending and borrowing protocol, seamlessly integrated into the Proof-of-Liquidity ecosystem. It enables users to lend assets to earn interest and BGT rewards, or borrow assets against collateral.

## Key Features

### Native to Berachain

Bend is built directly into Berachain's core infrastructure:

- **Gas Optimized**: Minimal transaction costs
- **PoL Integration**: Earn BGT through lending
- **HONEY Native**: Direct support for Berachain's stablecoin
- **High Performance**: Fast execution and settlement

### Proof-of-Liquidity Rewards

As part of PoL, Bend participants:

<Check>Earn BGT emissions on deposits</Check>
<Check>Participate in governance</Check>
<Check>Receive lending interest</Check>
<Check>Access capital-efficient borrowing</Check>

### Multiple Market Types

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Isolated Markets">
    Individual lending pools with separate risk parameters. Each market has its own collateral requirements and interest rates. Ideal for new or volatile assets.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Cross-Collateral Markets">
    Borrow against multiple assets simultaneously. Use your entire portfolio as collateral for maximum capital efficiency.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Stablecoin Markets">
    Optimized for HONEY and other stablecoins with lower collateral requirements and competitive rates.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How Bend Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Supply Assets">
    Deposit tokens into Bend lending pools
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Earn Interest">
    Receive interest from borrowers + BGT emissions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use as Collateral">
    Enable your deposits as collateral for borrowing
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Borrow">
    Borrow assets up to your collateralization limit
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Assets

### Collateral Assets

Assets you can deposit and use as collateral:

- **BERA** - Native gas token (75% LTV)
- **wBTC** - Wrapped Bitcoin (70% LTV)
- **wETH** - Wrapped Ethereum (80% LTV)
- **HONEY** - Native stablecoin (85% LTV)
- **LP Tokens** - BEX liquidity positions (60-70% LTV)

### Borrowable Assets

Assets available to borrow:

- HONEY (Native stablecoin)
- wBTC
- wETH
- BERA
- Various ecosystem tokens

<Tip>
  New assets are regularly added through governance votes. Check the [markets page](https://bend.berachain.com/markets) for the latest listings.
</Tip>

## Interest Rates

Bend uses dynamic interest rates based on utilization:

### Supply APY

```
Base APY: 2-15%
BGT Rewards: 5-50%
Total APY: 7-65% (depending on market)
```

### Borrow APR

```
Base APR: 3-20%
Efficiency Mode Discount: -1%
Total APR: 2-20% (depending on market)
```

<Note>
  Rates automatically adjust based on supply and demand. Higher utilization = higher rates.
</Note>

## Risk Parameters

| Asset | Max LTV | Liquidation Threshold | Liquidation Penalty |
|-------|---------|----------------------|-------------------|
| BERA | 75% | 80% | 5% |
| wETH | 80% | 85% | 5% |
| wBTC | 70% | 75% | 7% |
| HONEY | 85% | 90% | 3% |
| LP Tokens | 60% | 65% | 10% |

**LTV (Loan-to-Value)**: Maximum you can borrow against collateral
**Liquidation Threshold**: Point at which position can be liquidated
**Liquidation Penalty**: Fee paid to liquidators

## Safety Features

### Health Factor

Your position's safety is measured by the health factor:

```
Health Factor = (Collateral Ã— Liquidation Threshold) / Borrowed Amount

> 1.0 = Healthy
< 1.0 = Subject to liquidation
```

<Warning>
  Always maintain a health factor above 1.2 to avoid liquidation during market volatility.
</Warning>

### Isolation Mode

New or volatile assets are isolated:

- Can only borrow stablecoins
- Borrowing limit per account
- Cannot be combined with other collateral

### Oracle Protection

- Multiple oracle sources (Chainlink, BEX TWAP)
- Price deviation checks
- Circuit breakers for extreme volatility

## Bend vs Traditional Lending

| Feature | Bend | Traditional DeFi Lending |
|---------|------|------------------------|
| Chain Integration | Native | External dApp |
| PoL Rewards | Yes (BGT) | No |
| Gas Costs | Ultra-low | Variable |
| Collateral Options | Includes LP tokens | Usually limited |
| Interest Rates | Competitive | Variable |

## Use Cases

### For Lenders

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Passive Income">
    Earn yield on idle assets without active trading. Combine lending APY with BGT emissions for attractive returns.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="LP Token Lending">
    Earn triple yield: trading fees + BGT + lending interest by depositing LP tokens from BEX.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### For Borrowers

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Leverage Trading">
    Borrow to increase your trading position size without selling existing holdings.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Liquidity Without Selling">
    Access liquidity while maintaining exposure to your assets (e.g., borrow HONEY against BERA).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Yield Farming">
    Borrow assets to provide liquidity in high-APY pools, amplifying returns.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
